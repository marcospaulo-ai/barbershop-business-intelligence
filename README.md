# 📊 Barbershop Business Intelligence Dashboard

> Data analytics solution designed to track financial metrics, optimize operational hours, and extract strategic business insights for a real barbershop client.

## 📌 Project Overview
This dashboard was built to transform raw operational data from a barbershop into actionable business intelligence. The main goal is to support the business owner in making data-driven decisions regarding pricing, marketing hooks, and managing idle hours.

## ⚡ Key Metrics Tracked
- **Total Revenue (Faturamento):** R$ 13,000
- **Average Ticket (Ticket Médio):** R$ 52.00
- **Total Clients (Clientes):** 112 Unique Customers
- **Total Services (Atendimentos):** 255 Completed Appointments

## 🎯 Strategic Business Insights
Based on the data visualization, three critical business strategies were defined:
1. 📈 **Ticket Average Optimization:** Target to increase the current average ticket from R$ 52.00 to R$ 60.00.
2. 💈 **Hook Product:** "Corte" (Haircut) is identified as the best-selling service; used as a flagship to attract new clients.
3. 🛑 **Idle Time Mitigation:** Tuesdays and Wednesdays present lower volume (revenue loss). Special promotions can be deployed to populate these specific slots.

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Applied
- **Data Visualization:** Power BI (Layout structure, conditional coloring, and custom charts).
- **Business Analytics:** KPI definition, trend analysis, and strategic insight extraction.
